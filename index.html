What Is a Wellness Challenge?

If you’ve got a body or a mind, then you’ve bound to have experienced a wellness challenge – even if you may not recognise it as such!

We’ve categorised what we believe are the most common wellness challenges below. Some of them may overlap with each other. Ultimately, we want you to OWN your wellness challenges and work towards maintaining ‘good wellness.’

Hopefully the categories below will help you recognise what challenges you may be experiencing and subsequent articles from our ambassadors, as well as future trainings, will empower you to work through your own wellness challenges.

Mind Wellness

As we’ve evolved, so have our imaginations. Sometimes our minds cant’t distinguish between what’s objectively real or not and this can cause all kinds of challenges for us, such as anxiety and depression. You Own It is here to empower you to take control over your mind once again so that you can live a happy and fulfilled life.

Body Wellness

We can sometimes feel like our bodies are a ‘battlefield’ and we know deep inside that we ought to maintain a healthy body. You Own It is here to empower you to take control and make small changes in your life so that you can start to feel healthier but also to enjoy your journey towards becoming healthier.

Social Wellness

Sometimes, we can be surrounded by all sorts of people but we still can feel lonely. We find it easier to conform rather than following our own intuition and taking our own paths. You Own It is here to give you the confidence to go out there to find ‘your crowd.’ We will help you transform into the social butterfly you’re meant to be!

Everyday Wellness

Everyday Wellness challenges can affect us all. After all, they can happen every day! It could be argued that knowing how to own your Everyday Wellness challenges is the most important strategy you can take away from this website, as once you feel empowered to do so, the other wellness challenges may seem easier for you to work through.
